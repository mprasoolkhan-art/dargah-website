About the Dargah

The Dargah of Hazrath Khwaja Syed Shah Gulam Ali Shah R.A in Madanapalle, Andhra Pradesh, is a center of spirituality and peace. Devotees from all communities visit to seek blessings, healing, and guidance.

Main Dargah

History

Hazrath Khwaja Syed Shah Gulam Ali Shah R.A dedicated his life to serving humanity and spreading the message of peace, love, and devotion. His teachings continue to inspire thousands of devotees who visit his shrine every year.

Visiting Information

Address: PEDDA DARGAH road, Madanapalle, 517235, Annamayya district, A.P

Visiting Hours:

  • Morning: 6:00 AM – 10:00 AM
  • Evening: 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM

Practices & Events

  • Urs Celebration: Annual Urs attracts thousands of devotees, marked by prayer, qawwali, langar (food distribution), and spiritual gatherings.
  • Daily Prayers: Devotees offer prayers and seek spiritual healing.
  • Spiritual Guidance: A place for blessings and peace of mind.

Spiritual Significance

The Dargah stands as a beacon of unity where people of all faiths gather. Many devotees have experienced peace, answered prayers, and blessings after visiting. It is not only a religious site but also a place of communal harmony.
